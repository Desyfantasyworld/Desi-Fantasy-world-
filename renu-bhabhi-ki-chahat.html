Renu Bhabhi Ki Chahat

Gaav ki galiyaan uss din kuch zyada hi sunsaan thi. Garmi apne chaaram par thi aur log chhatt ke neeche chhup kar baith gaye the. Aise mein Raj apne bade bhai ke ghar aaya tha – summer vacation ke liye.

Renu bhabhi, ek sundar, naveen dulhan thi jinki shaadi ko sirf 6 mahine hue the. Gora rang, gehri aankhen aur lambi zulfein, unka persona hi kuch aisa tha ki Raj ki nazar unse hat'ti nahi thi.

Raj ne pehli baar dekha ki Renu bhabhi ne halki si sari pehni thi, jisme se unka kamar ka curve saaf dikh raha tha. Dil dhadak utha. Renu bhabhi ne muskurate hue kaha, “Raj, thak gaye ho kya? Aao, nimbu paani laati hoon.”

Us pal kuch alag hi tha... hawayein tez chal rahi thi aur dono ki bechaini bhi. Jab Renu bhabhi glass thama rahi thi, Raj ka haath unke haath se chhoo gaya... dono ne ek pal ke liye aankhon mein aankhon daal li.

Sham ko jab ghar mein koi nahi tha, bhai ka phone aaya ki unhe der ho jayegi... Renu bhabhi ne kaha, “Khaana tumhare sath hi khaungi Raj, akeli khane ka mann nahi karta.”

Raat mein bijli chali gayi... andhera aur garmi dono ka asar tha. Renu bhabhi chhat par thi, hawa lene... Raj bhi chhupke se chhat par gaya... dono chup the, lekin nazar sab kuch keh rahi thi.

Ek pal aaya jab dono paas aaye, Raj ne haath badhaya, aur Renu bhabhi ne roka nahi... wo pal, wo raat... ek naya rishta ban gaya... jo na jaayaz tha, na hi bilkul sahi... bas ek chahat thi, jo pura ho gaya.

Subah sab kuch waise ka waisa tha, lekin dono ki aankhon mein ek raaz tha – *Renu Bhabhi Ki Chahat* ka raaz.
